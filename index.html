About Marry.SG

A web-based wedding planning application. Equipped with simple tools, guides and an array of multi-racial Singapore-based vendors, start planning your Big Day with Marry.SG today!

Features

SHARE

Your plans and ideas with your significant other, your bridesmaids and groomsmen and more!

  1. Just one platform for all details to be stored at!
  2. Just one platform for all to visit, view plans and share ideas!
  3. No longer a need to send anything via email!
PLAN

Your Big Day with our help!


  1. Tools to help give you suggestions on what you should start doing all the way till your Big Day,
  2. Tips for you,
  3. And Singapore-based vendors for you to choose from!
MULTI-RACIAL

Marry.SG has guides and vendor suggestions for all the major races in Singapore; Chinese, Malays and Indians!

Widgets

Things to do
What should be done by when

Load a template done for you, or create your own! Plan out what needs to be done according to the number of months before your Big Day, and strike them off once you are done!


Wedding Notebook
For quick notes and lists

Use this to make a list of your favourite songs you would like to play on your Big Day, or to note down a pretty manicure design you came across!


Wedding Discover
Multi-racial Sg-based vendors

Provides you with an array of Singapore-based vendors to kick start your enquiries and shopping! And we did not forget to consider different races!


Guestlist Manager
Manage guests & allocate tables

Manage your guests, their addresses, and send e-invites to them via email. You can also start allocating guests to tables once most of them have RSVPed!


Wedding Day Itinerary
Plan & print the line up of events

Use this to help you plan out the line up of events on your Big Day! Need it printed out for your helpers and/or vendors? Sure! Just export it as a PDF!


Customisable RSVP
Your very own "website"

This is where your guests will go to RSVP for your wedding. Customise your canvas by adding in stories of how you met your significant other and share pictures from your pre-wedding photoshoots!
